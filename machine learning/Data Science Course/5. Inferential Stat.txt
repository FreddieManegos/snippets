What is distribution?
	Defn.
		A distribution is a function that shows the possible values for a variable and how often they occur.
	e.g.
		Rolling a die
		Probability of getting 1 = 1/6 ~ 0.17
		Rolling a die is an example of a |Discrete Uniform Distribution|, means that all outcomes has an equal chance of occuring
	Probability Distribution (Normal, Binomial, Uniform)
